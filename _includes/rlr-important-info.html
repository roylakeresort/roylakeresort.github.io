Important Information

  1. Reservations during May 20 thru September 10 may require a 4 night minimum stay. Call or email us for availability.
  2. All units require a Non-Refundable First-Night Deposit by:
    • cash (in person),
    • check (mail or in person),
    • or by credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express)
  3. All units include Air Conditioning, Heater, Full Bath, Full Kitchen, Pots, Pans, Utensils & Linens.
  4. You need to bring your own towels and soap for both bathing and cleaning the kitchen. If you forget, these items are available for purchase or for rent with a small cleaning fee in the main lodge.
  5. Check-In is 4pm
  6. Check-Out is 12pm
  7. Sleeper-sofas are provided in some of the cabins. These are best for young adults, teenagers, or kids.
  8. Cancellation policy - Remember all deposits are Non-Refundable however, if you make another reservation for the same year we can transfer that deposit to the new reservation/arrival date.
    • In addition to the Non-Refundable First-Night Deposit, we do have a 30-Day Cancellation Policy- Any Reservation cancelled within 30-Days of your arrival date is responsible for the ENTIRE stay or nights/units not rebooked. We will try to rebook your stay- any nights/units rebooked will no longer be your responsibility.
    • We do have an excellent rebooking rate, but a rebooking is not guaranteed.
    • Any reservation cancelled before the 30-Day notice will only be subject to forfeiting the already Non-Refundable First-Night Deposit.
    • The 30-Day CANCELLATION POLICY is only in effect for reservations with arrival dates from May 20th to September 30th. The Non-Refundable First-Night Deposit is in effect year-round.
