Welcome!

Roy Lake is in Northeastern South Dakota where the picturesque glacial lakes are commonplace. Where a beautiful place and an exciting place is found.

Roy Lake Resort & Lodge is open all year round for family fun and recreation as well as business and outdoor retreats.

We are the destination for family reunions and family fun located inside beautiful Roy Lake State Park.

There is excellent fishing all year round and waterfowl, pheasant, turkey & deer hunting.

Go view our Facebook photos!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION
