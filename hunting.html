Marshall County South Dakota provides ~55,000 acres of public hunting land and Roy Lake Resort & Lodge is in a prime location to access these acres of land!

The area has prime hunting for pheasant, waterfowl, and turkey.

Get your hunting license online.

We also provide hunting guide services!

Pheasant

Pheasant numbers done by the SD GF&P shows numbers in the Roy Lake area to be the one of the strongest in the state. The state has received a lot of water, but our elevation is so high at 1900 ft that the water drains off and doesn't disturb hatching.

Waterfowl

There is a lot of water to allow a lot of waterfowl to rest on their long trip south. We are in a perfect fly way for snow geese, blue geese, ducks, mallards, and swans.

Turkey

We have a burgeoning groups of turkeys in the area. They even walk and gobble inbetween the cabins. Instead of having Butterball or Jennie'o's turkey, why don't you bag your own?
