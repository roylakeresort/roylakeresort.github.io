The resort has boats for rent. We have one pontoon boat and some 14' aluminum hull boats. Both types are great for fishing, trolling, lake exploration, and family fun!

The rates below were last updated on January 1, 2015.

Pontoon - 40 HP

$259 / day - includes life jackets

Explore and relax on the lake with our pontoon boat! Enjoy the sun or setup the bimini for some welcomed shade! There is one pontoon boat and has a 40 HP Big Foot Mercury motor.

Aluminum 14' - 6 HP

$85 / day - includes life jackets

There are several 14' boats that have 6 HP Mercury motors. All motors are newer and maintained. These are perfect for fishing and trolling! Or even for a lake cruise! These boats will seat a maximum of 4 people, though an exception may be made by management for small children, though not guaranteed.
